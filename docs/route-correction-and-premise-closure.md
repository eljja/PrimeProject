# TICKET-126: Route Correction and Premise Closure

## Abstract

TICKET-126 audits the four infinite-bridge contracts of TICKET-125. It proves
three exact intermediate statements: a separation obstruction to one proposed
Riemann Hypothesis density route, an inverse-limit characterization of natural
Collatz finite-stopping counterexamples, and an explicit uniform bound for the
proper-prime-power contamination in the weighted Goldbach sum. It also reports
one preregistered finite Twin Prime holdout at `2^25`. The holdout passes its
frozen affine-contraction test, but supplies no uniform recurrence or exact
gap-two theorem. None of the four conjectures is proved or refuted.

TICKET-126은 TICKET-125에서 만든 네 개의 무한 연결 계약을 재검토한다.
이번 단계에서 정확히 증명한 것은 세 가지다. 첫째, 리만 가설을 위한 한
조밀성 경로가 성립할 수 없음을 분리 정리로 보였다. 둘째, 자연수 콜라츠
유한 하강 반례를 역극한 잉여류 경로로 정확히 특징지었다. 셋째, 가중
골드바흐 합에서 진소수 거듭제곱 항의 명시적 균일 상계를 증명했다. 또한
`2^25`까지 한 번의 사전등록된 쌍둥이 소수 홀드아웃을 수행했다. 이 유한
검사는 고정된 아핀 수축 조건을 통과했지만 균일 점화식이나 간격 2 정리를
주지 않는다. 네 난제는 모두 미해결이다.

## 2. Riemann Hypothesis: a density route is separated

### Theorem RH-126: `ContinuousEvaluationSeparatesAutocorrelationCone`

Let `V` be a real topological vector space of functions on a group, let `e` be
the identity, and assume evaluation `ell(h)=h(e)` is continuous. Let `C` be the
convex cone generated by real autocorrelations

```text
h = g * tilde(g),       tilde(g)(x) = g(x^(-1)).
```

If `V` contains an `f` with `f(e)<0`, then `C` is not dense in `V`.

### Proof / 증명

For every real autocorrelation,

```text
(g * tilde(g))(e) = integral g(x)^2 dx >= 0.
```

Therefore every element of `C` lies in the closed half-space
`ell^(-1)([0,infinity))`. Continuity of `ell` makes this half-space closed. If
`ell(f)<0`, then an open neighborhood of `f` is disjoint from `C`, so
`f` is not in the closure of `C`. Hence `C` is not dense. `QED`

한국어로 말하면, 자기상관 함수는 항등원에서 항상 `L2` 노름의 제곱이므로
음수가 될 수 없다. 항등원 값이 음수인 시험함수가 전체 공간에 하나라도
있으면 연속 평가함수가 두 집합을 닫힌 반공간으로 분리한다. 따라서
자기상관 원뿔이 전체 시험함수 공간에 조밀하다는 경로는 폐기해야 한다.

### Scope and correction / 적용 범위와 경로 수정

This theorem applies only when the proposed ambient space has continuous
evaluation at `e` and contains a negative-at-identity function. A smaller
moment-constrained admissible class may not contain such a separator. Thus the
result does not disprove Weil's criterion and does not locate a zeta zero off
the critical line.

폐기하는 것은 **전체 실수 시험함수 공간에서 자기상관 원뿔의 조밀성을
먼저 증명한 뒤 양성을 연장하는 우회로**다. 유지하는 목표는 정확한 Weil
허용 조건을 만족하는 모든 자기상관에 대해 명시공식의 이차형식이
비음수가 됨을 순환 논리 없이 직접 증명하는 것이다.


## 3. Collatz: natural paths inside the 2-adic tree

Use the accelerated odd Collatz map

```text
T_odd(n) = (3n+1) / 2^v2(3n+1).
```

For each precision `k`, let `U_k` contain the odd residue classes modulo `2^k`
for which the exact lifted-cylinder calculation has not certified an iterate
below the starting value before the consumed valuation reaches `k`.

### Theorem CO-126: `EventuallyLowUnresolvedPathIffFiniteStoppingCounterexample`

A positive odd integer `n` has no finite stopping descent if and only if its
compatible residue path is an eventually-low infinite path through the trees
`U_k`.

Here “eventually low” means that from some level onward the lift always chooses
the representative below `2^k`. Equivalently, the inverse-limit path eventually
stabilizes to one ordinary positive integer rather than only defining a
2-adic integer.

### Proof / 증명

Forward direction: choose `k` larger than the bit length of `n`. Its residue
representative is then exactly `n`, and every later refinement is the low
child. If the path left some `U_k`, the exact cylinder certificate would give a
finite iterate of `n` below `n`, contradicting the assumption.

Reverse direction: an eventually-low path is represented by a fixed positive
odd integer `n` after some level. If `n` descended after `m` accelerated steps,
let `S` be the total 2-adic valuation consumed by those steps. For every
sufficiently large `k>S`, the valuation word is fixed on the corresponding
cylinder and the exact calculation certifies the same descent. This
contradicts membership in all later `U_k`. `QED`

TICKET-125 proved that finite stopping descent for every `n>1` is equivalent
to the Collatz conjecture by strong induction. The fixed path `n=1` is itself
eventually low and has no strict iterate below one, so it must be excluded from
counterexample language. Combining the corrected statements gives:

```text
Collatz holds
iff every integer n>1 has finite stopping descent
iff the adaptive unresolved tree has no nontrivial eventually-low infinite path.
```

**TICKET-127 correction notice:** the first published TICKET-126 corollary
omitted the `n>1` qualifier and therefore stated the impossible target of
removing the permanent `n=1` path. The path-equivalence theorem remains valid;
only its Collatz corollary and next target required correction.

한국어 핵심은 “무한 잉여류 경로” 전체가 아니라 **결국 하나의 자연수로
고정되는 경로**만 배제하면 된다는 점이다. 임의의 2-adic 경로를 자연수
반례로 취급하던 논리적 과잉을 제거했다.

### Exact bounded audit / 정확한 유한 감사

| Precision | Odd classes | Unresolved | Unresolved mass | Longest observed low run |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 18 | 131,072 | 9,247 | 7.0549% | 14 |

The 28-bit audit is exact for its finite domain. The decreasing mass does not

statement is uniform nontrivial exclusion, not convergence suggested by a plot.

28비트에서 미해결 비율이 약 3%로 줄었어도 이것은 밀도 정보다. 예외가
하나도 없다는 전칭 명제와 다르다. 따라서 이 수치를 콜라츠 증명으로
외삽하지 않는다.

### Boundary-ray correction / 경계 광선 교정

The compatible ray `r_k=-3^(-1) mod 2^k` survives the determined first-word
boundary at every fixed precision, but it is not eventually low. If it
stabilized to a positive integer `n`, then `2^k` would divide `3n+1` for
arbitrarily large `k`, forcing `3n+1=0`, impossible in positive integers.

따라서 이 광선은 “고정 정밀도만으로 모든 원통을 닫을 수 없다”는 방법론적
반례로는 유지하지만 자연수 콜라츠 반례 후보에서는 폐기한다.

## 4. Goldbach: close the proper-prime-power term

Let

```text
G(N) = sum_(m=1)^(N-1) Lambda(m)Lambda(N-m),
```

and let `P(N)` be the part for which at least one coordinate is a proper prime
power `p^a`, `a>=2`. Let `Q(N)` count distinct proper prime powers at most `N`.

### Theorem GB-126A: proper-prime-power count

For `N>=4`,

```text
Q(N) <= sqrt(N) + (floor(log_2 N)-2) N^(1/3).
```

### Proof

For exponent `a=2`, there are at most `sqrt(N)` possible prime bases. For each
`a=3,...,floor(log_2 N)`, there are at most `N^(1/a)<=N^(1/3)` bases. Summing
these upper bounds proves the claim; duplicate powers only reduce the number
of distinct values. `QED`

### Theorem GB-126B: weighted contamination

Since each von Mangoldt product is at most `log(N)^2` and the union of the two
coordinate conditions contains at most `2Q(N)` ordered terms,

```text
P(N) <= 2 sqrt(N) log(N)^2
        * [1 + (log_2 N - 2) N^(-1/6)].
```

The derivative of `(log_2 x-2)x^(-1/6)` has the sign of
`6+2log(2)-log(x)`. Hence the bracket decreases above
`exp(6+2log(2)) < 1614`. At the verified finite limit
`H=4*10^18`, this yields the uniform constant

```text
B = 2.0949181787429647.
```

한국어로는, 진소수 거듭제곱의 개수를 지수별로 직접 세고 각 가중치가
`log(N)^2` 이하임을 이용해 오염 항 전체를 명시적으로 눌렀다. 이 부분은
이제 미증명 상수가 아니다.

### Endpoint consequence / 끝점 결과

TICKET-125 reduced the analytic-to-finite overlap to

```text
A - K/log(H) - B log(H)^2/sqrt(H) > 0.
```

At `H=4*10^18`, the normalized contamination is
`1.9217218512626284e-6`. If one could prove `A=1`, the strict admissible ceiling
would be `K<42.83274372223497`; `K=40` would leave margin
`0.06613487795363798`.

These `A` and `K` values are illustrations of the remaining budget, not proved
constants. The closed premise is `B`; the open premises are a uniform positive
major coefficient `A` and a uniform pointwise signed residual constant `K`.

`A=1`, `K=40`은 현재 증명된 값이 아니라 남은 예산을 보여주는 조건부
예시다. 이 구분을 생략하면 골드바흐가 해결됐다는 잘못된 결론이 된다.

```text

다음 정리: 명시적 골드바흐 주항 및 잔차 상수
```

## 5. Twin Prime: preregistered 32M falsification holdout

TICKET-125 proved only the conditional recurrence bridge: if

```text
Q(2X) <= (3/4) Q(X) + 23/100
```

holds uniformly on the dyadic tail, then `limsup Q(X)<=0.92` on that tail. The
coefficients were frozen before the 32M holdout.

### Preregistration and recovery history

1. Primary preregistration commit:
   `b7d5fb6bc10583d0243c66b41ea4cab315f40738`.
2. The first execution completed the expensive calculation but failed an
   aggregate reconstruction gate before any endpoint result was serialized.
   The gate had accidentally been tightened from the inherited `1e-7` to
   `1e-9`; no pass/fail endpoint was observed.
3. Recovery preregistration commit:
   `669b28ad8278844f9e9c1d9b2281a3582b3772d3`. It restored only the inherited
   reconstruction tolerance `1e-7`, retained the primary endpoint tolerance
   `1e-12`, forbade retuning, and allowed one recovery run.
4. The one valid recovery run was serialized before aggregate validation.

이 복구 절차는 결과를 본 뒤 계수를 바꾼 것이 아니다. `alpha=0.75`,
`beta=0.23`, 블록 선택, 홀드아웃 범위, 주 판정 허용오차는 그대로 유지됐다.
`retuned_after_result=false`가 데이터에 기록된다. 첫 실행은 결과 파일을
남기지 않았으므로 유효 시행으로 세지 않지만, 재현성 감사를 위해 실패
이력 자체를 공개한다.

### Finite result / 유한 결과

|---|---:|---:|
| exact `Q` | 0.802678282535945 | 0.743080655632258 |
| certified `Q` | 0.834379378078478 | 0.771657434492807 |


The preregistered finite transition passes. This is the fifth finite dyadic

parity problem, and does not prove a positive exact-gap-two lower bound.

사전등록 홀드아웃의 통과는 후보 점화식이 32M에서 반증되지 않았다는 뜻이다.
무한 꼬리 전체의 점화식, 임의의 `X`에 대한 보간, 체 방법의 parity barrier,

## 6. Route decisions / 경로 의사결정

| Problem | Keep / 유지 | Discard / 폐기 | Remaining decisive object / 남은 핵심 대상 |
|---|---|---|---|
| RH | direct non-circular positivity on every admissible autocorrelation | density in the full test space | exact Weil quadratic-form positivity |
| Collatz | eventually-low natural paths in the adaptive exact tree | arbitrary 2-adic and boundary-ray paths as natural counterexamples | uniform exclusion or one genuine eventually-low path |
| Goldbach | explicit endpoint budget with proved `B` | treating all `A,K,B` as equally unknown | replayable pointwise constants `A,K` |
| Twin | falsify a frozen recurrence on new scales | extrapolating five finite transitions | uniform Vaughan recurrence, interpolation, exact-gap positivity |

The strongest progress is not a conjecture proof. It is the removal of one RH
route that cannot work as stated, the exact identification of the only Collatz
inverse-limit paths relevant to natural counterexamples, and closure of one
Goldbach analytic premise. These corrections reduce search space and make the
next failure informative.

가장 강한 진전은 가설 해결을 선언하는 것이 아니라, 잘못된 탐색 공간을
줄이고 다음에 무엇을 증명하거나 반증해야 하는지 더 날카롭게 만든 것이다.
